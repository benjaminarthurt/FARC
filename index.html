About FARC

Founded in 1957, the Fulton Amateur Radio Club promotes amateur radio, emergency communications, and license training in Oswego County.

Learn More

Repeaters

We operate VHF and UHF repeaters serving Oswego County and participate in statewide linked systems.

Repeater Info

License Courses

FARC offers instruction for Technician and higher class licenses. Exams held monthly on the 4th Wednesday.

Emergency Comms

FARC members serve through RACES and SKYWARN, providing vital communications during emergencies.
