ARISS Program

February 27, 2024

So a few weeks ago I had my cousin over to help me with a few projects I couldn’t do myself. One was restringing my cable for my long wire antenna.

We got talking about amateur radio and school activities, then I told him about the ARISS program. We reviewed the current program and required documentation plus submitting dates. Well, he took the information to teachers at Oswego School District, and I believe we have a few that want to do a live chat with the ISS next fall.

Thus, I thought I should give everyone an update, and possibly notice that we might be getting a request to support a live chat session. I think this will be a wonderful opportunity for the club to promote Amateur Radio. Plus if anyone has a child, niece or nephew, or knows someone in any of the school districts, now is the time to share the information.
