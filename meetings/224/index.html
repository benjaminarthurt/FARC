Meeting Minutes — January 22, 2025

Call to Order

The meeting was called to order by President Ken Ellsworth (N2GFG) at 7:00 PM.

Approval of Previous Minutes

The minutes from the October meeting were read by the Secretary. Motion to approve: Dave Granoff; Seconded by: Rick Boutell. Motion carried.

Treasurer’s Report

Report by Rick Boutell. Total balance: $906.27. Motion carried.

Committee Reports

Field Day

  • Scheduled for June 28–29, 2025
  • Awaiting ARRL Rules and Regulations release
  • Awaiting report from Mike regarding Fort Ontario as a potential site

Education (Redd)

  • No General Class currently scheduled
  • If there is interest, a class could be organized by March
  • Proposed: Technician course in the fall, General course in the spring
  • Self-study support remains available for any license level

Public Service

  • Autism Run — may take place around St. Patrick’s Day (March)
  • Tour de Loop — may return in August
  • Fair Haven Triathlon — being discussed for September

Old Business

Christmas Party

Date: Saturday, December 13, 2025  •  Location: RFH  •  Time: 12:30 PM

Repeater Status

Current range remains limited due to height and power constraints. Open to future discussions for potential improvements.

New Business

Secretary Position

The club is seeking a replacement for the Secretary due to personal conflicts. A new Secretary will need to be in place by summer 2025.

Encouraging New/Younger Members

  • Boy & Girl Scouts, teachers, schools, and other community organizations
  • Collaboration with neighboring clubs: RAGS, LARC, QCWA, Bear Bait Radio Club
  • Participation in the Maker Faire in Syracuse

Remote Operation

Dave Granoff suggested using Google Remote Desktop for remote shack operation, allowing members to remotely access his station.

Next Meeting

Wednesday, February 26, 2025

Adjournment

Motion to adjourn: Ben; Seconded by: Ken. Meeting adjourned at 8:02 PM.

Educational Presentation

Presenter: Ben Townsend (AK2X)
Topic: Overview of the club website and social media functionality.
