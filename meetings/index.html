Meetings

Monthly meeting information and minutes

📅 Meeting Schedule

4th Wednesday of each month at 7:00 PM (no meetings July & August)
Oswego County Building, 200 North Second St. (Route 481), across from Mimi’s Restaurant, Fulton, NY

⚠️ Note: Future meeting locations are changing due to availability changes at the County Building. Stay tuned to this page or contact us to be added to the mailing list for the latest updates on meeting locations.

Plenty of parking — wheelchair accessible — no license required to attend!

Send ideas for meeting programs or to put on a presentation: contact a club member.

Past Meeting Topics

  • PSK31 digital modes
  • Slow scan television (SSTV)
  • Portable battery types
  • Mobile radio installation
  • Antenna and tower installation
  • Bat tracking via Amateur Radio
  • 50th Anniversary Special Event Station
  • Home brewing QRP HF Transceivers

Guest Speakers: Dave WJ2O, Ken N2ZN, Redd AI2N (DXpedition); Steve N2TXK (Data acquisition with rockets); Dave Eichorn 9WSYR (Weather in CNY); Keith WB2NVY (Communications on the Titanic); Bruce Frassinelli (Old Time Radio); Representatives from ARRL, ARES, RACES.
