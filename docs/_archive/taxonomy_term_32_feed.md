<span>QSL Cards from Around the World</span>
<div class="field field--name-body field--type-text-with-summary field--label-hidden field--item"><p>A QSL card is a written confirmation of a two-way radio communication between two amateur radio stations. A typical QSL card is the same size as a typical postcard and most are sent through the mail as such.</p></div>
