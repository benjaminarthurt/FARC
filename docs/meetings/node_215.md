Meeting Date
Wednesday, January 22, 2025 - 19:00
The Fulton Amateur Radio Club will hold its first business meeting of the year on:
**Date:** Wednesday, January 22, 2025
**Time:** 7:00 PM
**Location:** Oswego County Office Building
After the meeting, **Ben Townsend (AK2X)** will give a brief presentation on the content and functionality of the club's website and social media platforms. This presentation will highlight the features currently available to members and the broader amateur radio community.
Following the presentation, Ben will lead a discussion on ways the website and social media can be enhanced and better utilized to support the club’s activities, share information, and engage with members and the public.
All members are encouraged to attend, provide feedback, and share their ideas for improvements. This is a great opportunity to shape the future of the club's digital presence!
We look forward to seeing you there!
Presenter(s)
