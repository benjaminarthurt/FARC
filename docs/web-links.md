[QTH.com Equipment Swap](https://swap.qth.com/)
https://swap.qth.com/
is a dedicated marketplace for buying, selling, and trading amateur radio equipment. It's a trusted platform where amateur radio enthusiasts can connect to find new or used equipment.
[Getting Started in Amateur Radio W4NC](https://w4nc.com/getstarted/)
https://w4nc.com/getstarted/
is a helpful resource for those new to the amateur radio hobby. It provides easy-to-follow guides and tips to help beginners understand the basics and get on the air quickly.
[Cebik W4RNL Articles Collection](http://on5au.be/Cebik%20documents.html)
http://on5au.be/Cebik%20documents.html
offers a treasure trove of articles and technical writings by the renowned amateur radio expert W4RNL. Topics range from antenna design to propagation and station setup.
[Club Log](https://clublog.org/)
https://clublog.org/
is a powerful online tool for logging and analyzing amateur radio contacts. It provides tools for tracking DXCC, IOTA, and other awards, as well as propagation analysis and club member statistics.
[QRZ.com](https://www.qrz.com/)
https://www.qrz.com/
is a popular online resource for amateur radio operators. It includes a callsign database, forums, technical resources, and tools to connect operators around the world.
[DX Summit Spots](http://www.dxsummit.fi/#/)
http://www.dxsummit.fi/#/
is a real-time DX cluster platform. It allows users to spot and track amateur radio DX activity worldwide, helping enthusiasts find rare and interesting contacts.
[PSK Reporter](https://pskreporter.info/pskmap.html)
https://pskreporter.info/pskmap.html
is an online tool that visualizes digital mode activity in real-time. It helps operators understand propagation and see where their signals are being received.
[FCC License Search](https://wireless2.fcc.gov/UlsApp/UlsSearch/searchLicense.jsp)
https://wireless2.fcc.gov/UlsApp/UlsSearch/searchLicense.jsp
is an official tool provided by the Federal Communications Commission (FCC) to search for amateur radio licenses. It's an essential resource for verifying licenses or looking up license details.
[WA7BNM Contest Calendar](https://www.contestcalendar.com/)
https://www.contestcalendar.com/
This site provides detailed information about amateur radio contests throughout the world, including their scheduled dates/times, rules summaries, log submission information and links to the official rules as published by the contest sponsors.
[Upstate NY HAM Radio News & Information](https://www.upstateham.com/)
https://www.upstateham.com/
is a regional hub for amateur radio news and updates in Upstate New York. It features local events, repeater information, and resources for area operators.
[RF Safety Calculator](http://arrl.org/rf-exposure-calculator)
http://arrl.org/rf-exposure-calculator
is a tool provided by the ARRL to help amateur radio operators calculate safe RF exposure levels for their stations, ensuring compliance with FCC regulations.
[Basic Electronics Tutorials](http://www.electronics-tutorials.ws/index.html)
http://www.electronics-tutorials.ws/index.html
Basic electronics on-line tutorials
[Free Electronics "Textbooks"](http://www.allaboutcircuits.com/)
http://www.allaboutcircuits.com/
An excellent, free, on-line electronics reference! Lessons, experiments, work sheets (quizzes), videos, forums and blogs.
[Dave Granoff - K3AS](http://www.k3as.com/)
http://www.k3as.com/
Club President, Dave Granoff's Personal Website
[Ben Townsend AK2X](https://ak2x.com)
https://ak2x.com
Club Member Ben Townsend's personal website
[A Brief History of the Telegraph](https://www.history.com/topics/inventions/telegraph)
https://www.history.com/topics/inventions/telegraph
A history of Morse Code and the telegraph. Also provides many links to other good resources.
[Electronics Demonstrations](http://www.falstad.com/circuit/e-index.html)
http://www.falstad.com/circuit/e-index.html
Electronics Demonstrations - Java Based
[ARRL license manuals](https://www.arrl.org/ham-radio-license-manual)
https://www.arrl.org/ham-radio-license-manual
New License Manual Series - ARRL license manuals go the extra mile, with loads of advice to help you enjoy your new operating privileges.
[AA9PW FCC Exam Practice](https://www.aa9pw.com/amateur-radio-exam-practice/)
https://www.aa9pw.com/amateur-radio-exam-practice/
This site is intended to provide practice for the US Amateur Radio examinations required to obtain a US Amateur Radio licence (even learn morse code).

Study guide for the general class exam
