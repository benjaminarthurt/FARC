Are you a Technician Class amateur radio operator looking to expand your horizons? The Fulton Amateur Radio Club is excited to offer a free 10-week course aimed at helping you upgrade to a General Class Amateur (Ham) Radio license. This course will not only prepare you for the exam but also open up a world of new possibilities in your amateur radio journey.
**Course Details:**
* **Start Date:** Thursday, September 5
* **Time:** 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM
* **Location:** Lower level of the Oswego County Office Building, across from Mimi's Restaurant, Route 481, Fulton, NY
* **Class Dates:** September 5, 12, 19, 26; October 3, 10, 17, 24; November 7
* **Exam Date:** Thursday, November 14
**Why Upgrade to General Class?**
The General Class license is a significant step up from the Technician Class, granting you access to many more frequencies, including those that enable global communication without relying on the internet, cell towers, or repeaters. This license opens doors to advanced communication modes and technical opportunities, allowing you to explore the full potential of amateur radio.
Whether you’re interested in high-frequency (HF) operations, experimenting with digital modes, or understanding the solar effects on HF propagation, this course will provide you with the knowledge and skills needed. Topics will also cover operating with single sideband (SSB) on HF, utilizing test instruments, and delving into practical electronic circuits and antenna theory.
**What to Expect:**
The course is structured to build on what you’ve already learned as a Technician, with a deeper dive into the more complex aspects of amateur radio. Each class will cover a specific topic, ensuring that by the time you’re ready to take the exam, you’ll have a solid understanding of what it takes to be a successful General Class operator. The hands-on learning approach will make these concepts practical and easier to grasp, giving you the confidence to pass the exam and enjoy your new privileges.
**How to Register:**
To ensure we have enough resources prepared for all students, we ask that you preregister. You can do so by contacting Redd Swindells, AI2N, at ai2n(at)twcny.rr.com. Whether you’re looking to upgrade your license or simply expand your knowledge, this course is a great opportunity to connect with fellow radio enthusiasts and take your skills to the next level.
Don’t miss out on this chance to enhance your amateur radio experience. Sign up today and get ready to explore new frequencies and communication modes with your General Class license!
